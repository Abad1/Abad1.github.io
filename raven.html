Raven Landing Database Upgrade

Raven landing is a senior community center in Fairbanks, Alaska. During my senior year at UAF, me and a team of classmates met with the manager of Raven Landing to design a new database. The old database was done entirely with google sheets, and we were asked to create a more user friendly interface. Some of the things Raven Landing needed to keep records of include resident personal information, apartment information, waitlist information, and locks and keys.
