Hit Box Controller

Hit Box is a company that makes specialized controllers for fighting games. What makes them unique is the leverless design, which allows for more precision when performing complicated inputs. They run for about $250, and I was able to create my own for around $60. I rested my hands on a piece of paper and marked where my fingers were. Then I drilled holes into a plastic sheet for a button layout that fit my hands. I used Sanwa buttons and a Brooks arcade board for the components.
