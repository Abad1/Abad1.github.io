Hit Box Controller

Hit Box is a company that makes specialized controllers for fighting games. What makes them unique is the leverless design, which allows for more precision when performing complicated inputs. They run for about $250, and I was able to create my own for around $60. I rested my hands on a piece of paper and marked where my fingers were. The original was made of wood, but when I wanted to upgrade the buttons, it was too thick for them to fit. I then built an acrylic enclosure instead to house everything. The final product has Sanwa buttons and a Brooks arcade board.
